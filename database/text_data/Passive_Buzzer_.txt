Passive Buzzer Module

Description: A passive buzzer that requires an AC signal or PWM to produce sound. Unlike active buzzers, it can generate different frequencies and tones.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Frequency range: 1kHz to 10kHz
- Sound level: ~80dB at 10cm
- Current: ~15mA
- Control: PWM or tone signal

Applications:
- Musical tones
- Variable frequency alerts
- Sound effects
- Instrument simulation
- Audio feedback

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- I/O: PWM or tone control pin

Notes: Requires PWM or tone() function to generate sound. Can play melodies by varying frequency.
