IR Receiver Module (VS1838B)

Description: An infrared receiver module tuned to 38kHz for receiving IR signals from remote controls and IR transmitters. Demodulates and outputs digital data.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 2.7V to 5.5V
- Reception frequency: 38kHz
- Reception range: Up to 18 meters
- Output: Digital (demodulated signal)
- Carrier detection: Automatic

Applications:
- Remote control decoding
- IR communication
- Home automation
- TV/AC remote control
- Security systems

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Compatible with standard IR protocols (NEC, RC5, RC6). Requires library for decoding specific protocols.
