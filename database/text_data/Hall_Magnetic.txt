Hall Magnetic Sensor (Digital)

Description: A digital Hall effect sensor that detects magnetic fields and switches output state when a magnet is nearby. Used for proximity and position sensing.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Detection range: 1cm to 3cm
- Response time: < 10μs
- Polarity sensitive: Detects specific magnetic pole

Applications:
- Speed measurement (RPM)
- Position sensing
- Proximity switches
- Flow meters
- Security systems

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Output goes LOW when magnet is detected (varies by model). LED indicator shows detection state.
