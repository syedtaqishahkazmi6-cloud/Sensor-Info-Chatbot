Light Cup (Mercury Tilt) Sensor

Description: A tilt sensor using a small mercury ball that makes electrical contact when tilted. Similar to ball switch but with different form factor.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Tilt angle: ~30° from vertical
- Contact resistance: < 100Ω
- Max current: 100mA

Applications:
- Orientation detection
- Anti-theft devices
- Game controllers
- Safety switches
- Robotics

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Contains mercury - handle with care and dispose properly. Ball switch alternatives are safer.
