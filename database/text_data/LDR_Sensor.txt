LDR (Light Dependent Resistor) Sensor

Description: A photoresistor that changes resistance based on light intensity. Resistance decreases as light intensity increases. Used for light detection and measurement.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Resistance range: 1MΩ (dark) to 10KΩ (bright)
- Response time: ~20ms
- Spectral response: 520nm to 850nm
- Max power: 100mW

Applications:
- Light sensing
- Automatic lighting control
- Day/night detection
- Camera exposure control
- Solar tracking systems

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output (voltage divider with LDR)

Notes: Typically used in a voltage divider circuit. Output voltage varies inversely with light intensity.
