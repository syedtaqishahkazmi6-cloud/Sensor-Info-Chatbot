Flame Detection Sensor

Description: An infrared flame sensor that detects the presence of fire by sensing infrared light emitted by flames. Used for fire detection and safety systems.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Detection range: Up to 100cm (depends on flame size)
- Detection angle: ~60°
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Response time: < 20ms

Applications:
- Fire alarm systems
- Industrial safety
- Gas leak detection
- Automatic fire suppression
- Robot fire fighting

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Detects infrared light in the 760nm to 1100nm range, typical of flame emissions.
