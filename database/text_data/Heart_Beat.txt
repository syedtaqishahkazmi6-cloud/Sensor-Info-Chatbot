Heartbeat/Pulse Sensor Module

Description: A photoplethysmography (PPG) sensor that measures heart rate by detecting blood volume changes through light absorption. Uses an LED and photodetector.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Analog voltage
- Heart rate range: 30 to 240 BPM
- LED wavelength: Typically 660nm (red)
- Current: ~4mA

Applications:
- Heart rate monitoring
- Fitness trackers
- Medical devices
- Stress monitoring
- Biometric authentication

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output to ADC pin

Usage: Place finger on LED/sensor area. Signal varies with blood pulse. Requires signal processing for accurate BPM calculation.
