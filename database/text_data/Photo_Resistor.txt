Photoresistor (LDR) Sensor Module

Description: A light-dependent resistor module that outputs analog voltage based on ambient light levels. Resistance decreases as light intensity increases.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Resistance range: 1MΩ (dark) to 10KΩ (bright)
- Output: Analog voltage
- Response time: ~20ms
- Spectral response: 520nm to 850nm

Applications:
- Automatic lighting control
- Day/night detection
- Camera exposure
- Solar tracking
- Light meters

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output to ADC pin

Notes: Module includes voltage divider circuit. Output voltage decreases as light increases.
