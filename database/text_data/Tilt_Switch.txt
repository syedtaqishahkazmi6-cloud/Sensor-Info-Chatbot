Tilt Switch Sensor

Description: A tilt sensor that detects orientation changes. Uses a conductive ball or mercury that makes contact when tilted beyond a specific angle.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Tilt angle: 30° to 45° from horizontal
- Contact resistance: < 100Ω
- Max current: 100mA

Applications:
- Orientation detection
- Anti-theft alarms
- Safety devices
- Game controllers
- Robotics (balance)

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Ball switch types are safer than mercury versions. Detects tilt in one direction only.
