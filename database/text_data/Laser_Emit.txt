Laser Emitter Module

Description: A laser diode module that emits a focused red laser beam. Used for alignment, measurement, and optical applications.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Wavelength: 650nm (red)
- Output power: < 5mW (Class IIIa)
- Beam diameter: ~3mm
- Current: ~30mA

Applications:
- Laser alignment
- Distance measurement
- Optical communication
- Security systems
- Educational demonstrations

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- SIGNAL: Digital control (optional on some modules)

Safety: Never point laser at eyes. Class IIIa laser - avoid direct eye exposure.
