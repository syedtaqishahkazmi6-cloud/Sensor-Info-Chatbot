Temperature and Humidity Sensor (DHT11/DHT22)

Description: A combined digital temperature and humidity sensor. Provides calibrated digital output via single-wire interface.

Specifications:
- Temperature range: 0°C to 50°C (DHT11), -40°C to 80°C (DHT22)
- Humidity range: 20% to 90% RH (DHT11), 0% to 100% RH (DHT22)
- Accuracy: ±2°C temp, ±5% humidity (DHT11)
- Sampling rate: Once per 2 seconds
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V

Applications:
- Weather stations
- Home automation
- HVAC systems
- Greenhouses
- Data logging

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- DATA: Digital data pin (requires 10K pull-up)

Notes: DHT22 has better accuracy and range. Cannot read faster than once every 2 seconds.
