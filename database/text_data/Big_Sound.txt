Big Sound Sensor (High Sensitivity Microphone)

Description: A high-sensitivity sound detection module with an electret microphone and amplification circuit. Detects sound levels and outputs analog/digital signals.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Both analog and digital
- Sensitivity: Adjustable via potentiometer
- Frequency response: 20Hz to 20kHz
- Detection range: Up to several meters

Applications:
- Sound activated switches
- Voice recognition systems
- Noise monitoring
- Security alarms
- Audio level meters

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- AOUT: Analog output (sound level)
- DOUT: Digital output (threshold triggered)

Adjustment: Use the potentiometer to set the sound threshold for digital output.
