Two-Color LED Module

Description: A bicolor LED that can display two different colors (typically red and green) or a blend of both. Used for status indication and visual feedback.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- LED type: 5mm bicolor LED
- Colors: Red and Green
- Control: 2 digital pins
- Current: ~10mA per color

Applications:
- Status indicators
- Direction indicators
- Warning lights
- User feedback
- Debug indicators

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- R: Red control pin
- G: Green control pin

Notes: Both colors ON creates yellow/orange. Can use PWM for brightness control and color mixing.
