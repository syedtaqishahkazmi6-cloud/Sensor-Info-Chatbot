Soil Moisture Sensor

Description: A capacitive soil moisture sensor that measures water content in soil. More reliable and corrosion-resistant than resistive soil sensors.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Analog voltage
- Measurement range: 0-100% (dry to wet)
- Response time: < 1 second
- Corrosion resistant: Yes

Applications:
- Plant watering systems
- Agriculture monitoring
- Automatic irrigation
- Greenhouse control
- Soil research

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output to ADC pin

Notes: Higher voltage = drier soil, lower voltage = wetter soil. Calibrate for specific soil types. Capacitive design prevents corrosion.
