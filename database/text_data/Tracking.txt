Line Tracking Sensor Array

Description: An infrared line tracking sensor for detecting lines and paths. Typically consists of multiple IR sensors for line-following robots.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Number of sensors: 3 to 8 (varies)
- Output: Digital per sensor
- Detection range: 1-2cm
- Works on: White lines on dark surface or vice versa

Applications:
- Line-following robots
- Path tracking
- Autonomous vehicles
- Industrial automation
- Maze solving robots

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT1, OUT2, OUT3...: Digital outputs for each sensor

Notes: Each sensor provides independent output. Adjust potentiometers for sensitivity. Works best with high-contrast lines.
