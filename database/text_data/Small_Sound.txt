Small Sound Sensor (Microphone)

Description: A compact sound detection module with electret microphone. Detects sound levels and outputs analog/digital signals for audio sensing applications.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Both analog and digital
- Sensitivity: Adjustable via potentiometer
- Frequency response: 20Hz to 20kHz
- Detection range: Up to 2 meters

Applications:
- Sound activation
- Clap detection
- Voice activity detection
- Noise monitoring
- Audio triggers

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- AOUT: Analog output (sound level)
- DOUT: Digital output (threshold triggered)

Adjustment: Potentiometer sets the threshold for digital output. Analog provides continuous level measurement.
