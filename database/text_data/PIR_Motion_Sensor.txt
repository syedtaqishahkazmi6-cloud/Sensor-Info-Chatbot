PIR (Passive Infrared) Motion Sensor

Description: A passive infrared sensor that detects motion by sensing changes in infrared radiation emitted by humans and animals. Used for security and automation.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 4.5V to 20V
- Detection range: Up to 7 meters
- Detection angle: 110°
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Delay time: Adjustable 5-200 seconds

Applications:
- Security systems
- Automatic lighting
- Motion-activated cameras
- Home automation
- Energy saving systems

Wiring:
- VCC: 5V to 12V (check module)
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Adjustments: Two potentiometers control sensitivity and delay time. Requires 1-2 minutes to stabilize after power-on.
