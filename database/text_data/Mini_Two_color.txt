Mini Two-Color LED Module

Description: A compact two-color (typically red/green) LED that can display either color or a blend of both. Used for status indication.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- LED type: 3mm bicolor LED
- Colors: Red and Green (can mix to yellow/orange)
- Control: 2 digital pins
- Current: ~10mA per color

Applications:
- Status indicators
- Direction indicators
- Warning lights
- User feedback
- Debug indicators

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- R: Red control pin
- G: Green control pin

Notes: Both colors ON simultaneously creates yellow/orange. Can be PWM controlled for brightness.
