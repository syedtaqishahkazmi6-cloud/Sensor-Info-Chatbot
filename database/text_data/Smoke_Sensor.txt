Smoke Sensor (MQ-2)

Description: A gas sensor sensitive to smoke, flammable gases, and LPG. Uses a heated metal oxide semiconductor that changes resistance based on gas concentration.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 5V (heater requires 5V)
- Sensitivity: Smoke, LPG, Propane, Methane
- Output: Analog voltage
- Response time: < 10 seconds
- Heater power: ~800mW

Applications:
- Smoke detectors
- Gas leak detection
- Fire alarm systems
- Industrial safety
- Air quality monitoring

Wiring:
- VCC: 5V (required for heater)
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output to ADC pin

Notes: Requires 24-48 hour pre-heating for accurate readings. Output voltage increases with gas concentration.
