IR Emission (Transmitter) Module

Description: An infrared LED transmitter module that emits IR light at 38kHz frequency. Used with IR receivers for remote control and communication applications.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Emission frequency: 38kHz (standard)
- Wavelength: 940nm
- Transmission range: Up to 10 meters
- Modulation: Built-in oscillator

Applications:
- Remote controls
- IR communication
- Proximity sensing
- Beam break detection
- Data transmission

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- SIGNAL: Digital control pin

Notes: Requires modulation at 38kHz for standard IR receivers. Can be controlled with PWM for various protocols (NEC, RC5, etc.).
