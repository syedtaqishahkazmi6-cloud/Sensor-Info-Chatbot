Ball Tilt Switch Sensor

Description: A mercury-free ball tilt switch that detects tilt or orientation changes. Contains a conductive ball that makes contact when tilted beyond a certain angle.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Tilt angle: Typically 30° to 45° from horizontal
- Contact resistance: < 100Ω
- Max current: 100mA

Applications:
- Orientation detection
- Anti-theft alarms
- Game controllers
- Safety devices
- Robotics (balance detection)

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: The switch closes (conducts) when tilted in the direction of the ball movement.
