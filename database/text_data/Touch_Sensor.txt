Touch Sensor (Capacitive)

Description: A capacitive touch sensor that detects human touch through a metal pad. Uses the TTP223 touch controller IC for reliable touch detection.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 2.0V to 5.5V
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Response time: < 100ms
- Detection: Touch through thin materials
- Low power: Yes

Applications:
- Touch switches
- User interfaces
- Control panels
- Security systems
- Interactive displays

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Output goes HIGH when touched, LOW when released. Can detect touch through plastic, glass, or wood (up to 3mm thick).
