Linear Hall Effect Sensor

Description: A linear Hall effect sensor that outputs an analog voltage proportional to the magnetic field strength. Provides continuous measurement rather than simple on/off detection.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Analog voltage (0 to VCC)
- Sensitivity: Typically 1.3mV/G
- Magnetic range: ±100 to ±400 Gauss
- Bandwidth: Up to 30kHz

Applications:
- Current sensing
- Position measurement
- Magnetic field mapping
- Motor control
- Scientific instruments

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output to ADC pin

Notes: Output is VCC/2 with no magnetic field. Voltage increases/decreases with magnetic field polarity and strength.
