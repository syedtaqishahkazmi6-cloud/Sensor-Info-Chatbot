Digital Temperature Sensor (DHT11/DHT22)

Description: A digital temperature and humidity sensor that provides calibrated digital output. Uses a single-wire digital interface for easy integration with microcontrollers.

Specifications:
- Temperature range: 0°C to 50°C (DHT11), -40°C to 80°C (DHT22)
- Humidity range: 20% to 90% RH
- Accuracy: ±2°C temperature, ±5% humidity
- Sampling rate: Once every 2 seconds
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V

Applications:
- Weather stations
- Home automation
- Greenhouse monitoring
- HVAC systems
- Data logging

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- DATA: Digital data pin (requires 10K pull-up resistor)

Notes: Cannot be read faster than once every 2 seconds. DHT22 offers better accuracy and range than DHT11.
