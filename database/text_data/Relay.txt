Relay Module

Description: An electromechanical relay that allows a microcontroller to switch high-voltage/high-current devices. Provides electrical isolation between control and load circuits.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V (control)
- Switch rating: 10A at 250VAC / 10A at 30VDC
- Contact type: SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw)
- Trigger level: Active LOW or HIGH
- Isolation: Optical (on some modules)

Applications:
- Controlling AC appliances
- High-power DC switching
- Light control
- Motor control
- Home automation

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- IN: Control pin (digital)
- COM: Common terminal
- NO: Normally Open
- NC: Normally Closed

Notes: COM connects to NO when relay is activated, to NC when deactivated. Use flyback diode for inductive loads.
