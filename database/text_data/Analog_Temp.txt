Analog Temperature Sensor (LM35/TMP36)

Description: An analog temperature sensor that outputs a voltage linearly proportional to the Celsius temperature. Easy to use with microcontrollers' analog-to-digital converters.

Specifications:
- Temperature range: -40°C to +110°C (varies by model)
- Operating voltage: 2.7V to 5.5V
- Output: 10mV per degree Celsius (LM35)
- Accuracy: ±0.5°C at room temperature
- Response time: < 2 seconds in still air

Applications:
- Temperature monitoring
- Environmental control systems
- Battery thermal management
- Weather stations
- HVAC systems

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output to ADC pin

Formula: Temperature (°C) = (Vout × 1000) / 10 for LM35
