Analog Hall Effect Sensor

Description: An analog Hall effect sensor that detects magnetic fields and outputs a voltage proportional to the magnetic field strength. It can detect both north and south magnetic poles.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Analog voltage (0 to VCC)
- Detection range: Up to several centimeters depending on magnet strength
- Sensitivity: Detects changes in magnetic field intensity

Applications:
- Position sensing
- Speed detection (rotary applications)
- Proximity detection
- Current sensing
- Motor control systems

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Analog output to microcontroller ADC pin

Notes: The output voltage increases with magnetic field strength from one pole and decreases with the opposite pole.
