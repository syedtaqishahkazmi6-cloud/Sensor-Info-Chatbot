Infrared Obstacle Avoidance Sensor

Description: An infrared obstacle detection sensor that emits IR light and detects reflections from nearby objects. Used for obstacle avoidance in robotics and automation.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Detection range: 2cm to 30cm (adjustable via potentiometer)
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Detection angle: < 15°
- Response time: < 10ms

Applications:
- Robot obstacle avoidance
- Line following robots
- Automatic doors
- Security systems
- Industrial automation

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Adjustment: Use the onboard potentiometer to adjust detection sensitivity and range.
