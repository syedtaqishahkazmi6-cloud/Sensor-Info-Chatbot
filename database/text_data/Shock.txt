Shock/Vibration Sensor

Description: A spring-based vibration sensor that detects impacts and vibrations. The spring makes contact with the center terminal when shaken or impacted.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Output: Digital (HIGH/LOW)
- Sensitivity: Adjustable via spring tension
- Response time: < 10ms
- Contact resistance: < 100Ω

Applications:
- Impact detection
- Anti-theft alarms
- Vibration monitoring
- Game controllers
- Security systems

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Spring vibrates on impact, making momentary contact. Can detect taps, shakes, and vibrations.
