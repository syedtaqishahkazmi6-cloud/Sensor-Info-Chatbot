Reed Switch Sensor

Description: A magnetic reed switch that closes when a magnet is brought near. Consists of two ferromagnetic contacts in a sealed glass tube.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V (for logic)
- Contact rating: Up to 500mA at 200VDC
- Detection range: 1cm to 3cm
- Response time: < 2ms
- Form factor: Glass tube

Applications:
- Door/window sensors
- Proximity detection
- Security systems
- Flow meters
- Position sensing

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Normally open (NO) type closes when magnet approaches. Can be used as normally closed (NC) with different wiring.
