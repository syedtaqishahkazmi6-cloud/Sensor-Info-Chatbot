Active Buzzer Module

Description: An active buzzer that generates sound when a DC voltage is applied. Contains an internal oscillator circuit, so only a simple HIGH signal is needed to produce sound.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V
- Sound frequency: ~2kHz (fixed)
- Sound level: ~85dB at 10cm
- Current draw: ~20mA
- Control: Digital (HIGH/LOW)

Applications:
- Alarm systems
- Notification alerts
- Game sound effects
- Warning indicators
- User interface feedback

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- I/O: Digital control pin (HIGH = ON, LOW = OFF)

Notes: Unlike passive buzzers, active buzzers have a fixed frequency and cannot be modulated for different tones.
