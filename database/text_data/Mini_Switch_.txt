Mini Limit Switch

Description: A small mechanical limit switch that detects physical contact. Used as a position sensor or end-stop in mechanical systems.

Specifications:
- Operating voltage: 3.3V to 5V (for logic)
- Contact rating: Up to 5A at 250VAC
- Actuation force: ~50g
- Travel: ~2mm
- Output: Digital (normally open/closed)

Applications:
- End-stop detection
- Position sensing
- Safety interlocks
- 3D printers
- CNC machines

Wiring:
- VCC: 3.3V or 5V
- GND: Ground
- OUT: Digital output to microcontroller

Notes: Can be wired as normally open (NO) or normally closed (NC). Mechanical contact provides reliable detection.
